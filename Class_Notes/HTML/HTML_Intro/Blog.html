How to make lipton milk tea

Craving milk tea? Whip up your own delicious treat at home!

Who says you need a fancy shop to enjoy delicious milk tea? Here's a simple guide to crafting this delightful treat at home.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Lipton tea bag
  • 250ml (1 cup) hot water
  • 100ml (1/2 cup) milk
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Steep the tea: immerse the Lipton tea bag in the hot water for 3-5 minutes, or until the tea reaches your desired strength.
  2. Sweeten the tea: remove the tea bag and stir in the sugar until it dissolves completely.
  3. Combine with milk: pour the sweetened tea into a glass or mug filled with ice cubes (if using). Add the milk and stir gently to combine.
  4. Adjust and enjoy: taste the milk tea and adjust the sugar or milk quantity to your preference. Enjoy your homemade Lipton milk tea!
